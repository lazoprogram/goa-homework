🐠 Welcome to the World of Fish 🐟

Fish are aquatic animals that live in oceans, rivers, and lakes. They come in many colors, shapes, and sizes!

Popular Types of Fish

  • Goldfish – A common pet fish, easy to care for.
  • Clownfish – Famous from the movie *Finding Nemo*.
  • Betta Fish – Known for their bright colors and flowing fins.
  • Shark – A big and powerful fish found in the ocean.

    • Fun Facts 🧠

    🐡 Fish have been on Earth for more than 500 million years!

    🌊 Some fish can live in both fresh and salt water.

    👁️‍🗨️ Fish can see in color, just like humans.
